Weather & Kit

What to expect in Scotland in June — and how to prepare.

Scotland in June ☁️ 🌦 🌤

June is one of Scotland's better months — long daylight hours (up to 17–18 hrs in Edinburgh), temperatures typically between 10–18°C, but expect the unexpected. Rain can arrive anytime, particularly in the Highlands. Wind picks up on high ground and near the coast. Pack for all four seasons in a single day — it's a Scottish rite of passage.

🌡
Temperature10–18°C average. Evenings cooler. Pack layers — a light jumper even in sunshine.
🌧
RainfallEdinburgh is drier than the west. Expect ~8–10 rainy days in the month. A compact waterproof is essential.
☀
DaylightSunrise around 4:30am, sunset ~10pm near midsummer. Brilliant for long evening walks.
🦟
MidgesTiny biting insects, active June–August, especially near water and at dusk. Bring repellent if venturing outdoors.
👟
FootwearWaterproof walking shoes/boots recommended. City cobblestones get slippery in rain.
🔆
UVScotland's latitude means strong UV even on overcast days — SPF sunscreen still recommended.
